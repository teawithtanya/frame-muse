About Frame Muse

Meet the photographers behind the lens.

Alex Chen

Landscape & Nature Photographer

Alex is a passionate landscape photographer with over a decade of experience capturing the world's most breathtaking natural wonders. Based in the Pacific Northwest, Alex specializes in mountain photography and has traveled to over 30 countries documenting remote wilderness areas. Their work focuses on the intersection of light, weather, and dramatic terrain, creating images that transport viewers to places few have seen.

Sara Martinez

Portrait & Street Photographer

Sara brings stories to life through intimate portraits and candid street photography. With a background in photojournalism, Sara has a unique ability to capture authentic moments that reveal the human experience. Their work has been featured in numerous publications and exhibitions worldwide. Sara believes that every face tells a story, and their mission is to preserve those stories through powerful imagery that connects us all.
